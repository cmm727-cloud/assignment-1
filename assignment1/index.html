The Location of Sag Harbor

Sag Harbor is located on the eastern part of Long Island. There are bays and oceans about 10 minutes from the small town. It is isolated and far from malls and department stores.

The Small Knit Community of Sag Harbor

The town of Sag harbor is small and tight knit. Locals know locals, its amazing. There is one public school in Sag Harbor and I graduated with 70 students .

What Makes Sag Harbor Special

  1. Marinas and boats
  2. Festivals and entertainment
  3. Peaceful parks and nature (greenery)
